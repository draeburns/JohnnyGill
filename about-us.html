Chase Your Dreams

It is never too late to change your path.

I hope that the takeaway for the visitors of this site is empowerment. Unexpected life events can cause us to alter or abandon our dreams. I decided like most that if I wanted to change my life then I must push forward through adversity and fear by taking a leap of faith. I started by enrolling into Dallas College and completing my Associate of Science degree. The next step was to take a web design class which is a requirement for earning the Web Development Certificate.

The first assignment was to create a Blog website by using an avatar. I chose the avatar, Johnny Gill, because he is a talented R&B artist that understands the importance of chasing one's dream. Although, he was blessed with a heavenly voice he experienced moments of disappointment with the status of his career not progressing as he once anticipated. His story serves as a reminder to all that we must not allow unplanned events to deter us from our goals. Instead, we need to provide our support to others, strive to adjust to change, and work very hard to keep our dreams alive.

This class introduced me to the wonderous world of web development. Initially, I felt a bit overwhelmed with the process of learning the semantics involved in building webpages using HTML and CSS. My professor encouraged the class to utilize the services of Git and GitHub. The idea of sharing my projects publicly was intimidating and took me out of my comfort zone. Nevertheless, my passion to learn new concepts has caused me to embrace this new skill and work effortlessly building my developer skillset. The lessons learned in this class include how to use the text editor, Visual Studio Code; adaptive and responsive design, and the open source plaform, FileZilla FTP application.

This knowledge will serve instrumental in receiving my Web Development Certificate and then being able to apply for an Entry Level Full Stack Web Developer position.

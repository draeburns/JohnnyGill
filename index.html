JOHNNY GILL

Welcome to Johnny Gill's Fan Club

Bio

Johnny Gill Jr is an American singer and composer. Born on May 22, 1966 in Washington D.C. to parents Annie Mae and Johnny Gill Sr. He has 3 siblings Bobby, Jeff, and Randy. At the early age of 5, little Johnny's desire to perform began to blossom as he sung in his father's church choir. The siblings formed a group named Little Johnny and "Wings of Faith". Johnny's love for music led him to hone his gift by attending the Duke Ellington School of the Arts in Washington D.C.

Johnny Gill recorded his first demo at the age of 16 which led to a recording contract with Cotillion Records, a division of Atlantic Records. In 1983, his first album hit the market therefore, launching his music career as a R&B solo artist. Johnny is known for his deep baritone and sexy voice that is guaranteed to make a lady weak in the knees. His most recent projects has afforded him the opportunity to change up his sound to include a new jack swing vibe. Forty-One years in the music industry and Johnny Gill shows no signs of slowing down.

- Johnny Gill (1966-) by Otis Alexander. Written on January 27, 2022.

Award-Winning Collaborations

On January 24, 1984, Johnny Gill performed a duet with Stacy Lattisaw called "Perfect Combination". In 1988 he joined the singing group, New Edition, due to Bobby Brown's desire to pursue a solo career which resulted in a vacancy that Johnny Gill was elated to fill. This new union allowed Johnny the opportunity to benefit from the recording of the multi-platinum "Heart Break" album. In 1997, Johnny Gill collaboration with Gerald Levert and Keith Sweat led to the formation of LSG. In 2003, the group's second and final album "LSG2" was released but Unfortunately the untimely death of Gerald Levert on November 10, 2006, caused the trio to disband.

Johnny Gill has received two Grammy Award nominations: one with New Edition and one as a solo artist.

He has been nominated numerous times for various awards which include the NAACP Image Award, the Soul Train Award, the American Music Award, and two Grammy Awards. Johnny Gill has also appeared in a number of plays, movies, and television shows. Although the roles were minor the performances that he delivered were Award-winning. Johnny Gill has composed music for himself and some other entertainers.
